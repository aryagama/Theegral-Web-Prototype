Title
“Karya”

Nama Pembuat

Growing up in a nomadic family with a tendency towards individualism has shaped
connections that are not always explicit, yet remain palpable. It is not their clarity
that matters, but rather their existence, which feels more like a flow with time.
Amidst the hustle and bustle, changing places, and faces that come and go, a desire
to understand emerges—not to settle, but to keep moving forward.

Exhibited on Selasar Sunaryo Art Space: Senang Bersamamu (2025).

Mix Media

21 x 10 x 8 cm

2025

Photographer: Yoga Septi Irawan (SSAS Documentation)

We acknowledge the Traditional Custodians of the land on which we live and work, and pay our respects to Elders past, present and emerging. We recognise their continuing connection to land, waters and culture.
